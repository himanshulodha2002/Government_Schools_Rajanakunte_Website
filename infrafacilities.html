INFRASTRUCTURE AND FACILITIES

Some of our major facilities that we provide for the overall growth of a student. Multiple activities for a student to explore their extra curricular interests.

Auditorium
A Multifunctional auditorium to facilitate major events and assemblies.
Swimming Pool
An outdoor swimming pool for physical endurance.
Indoor Badminton Court
A fully furnished court for expert level training and practices.
Smart Class
Advanced teaching methods through smart classes for the better visualisation of students.

Food
Hygienic and nutritious food for the physical growth of students.

Fully functional and advanced laboratories for practical understanding of the subjects.

Park

Cleanliness
A major importance is given to the maintenance and cleaning of the campus.
