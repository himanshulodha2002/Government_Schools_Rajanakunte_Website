Our Vision and Mission


Vision: To provide quality education to students coming from all strata of society. To shape the life of each student for a better future.

Mission: To create a technologically upgraded institution which caters to the educational standards of today’s times.

Chairperson

Shri S R Vishwanath

MLA, Yelahanka | Member, TTD Board | Former Chairman, Bangalore Development Authority (BDA)

Singanayakanahalli Ramaiah Vishwanath is an Indian Politician who is the current MLA from Yelahanka constituency since 25 May 2008. He is the former Chairman of Bangalore Development Authority and presently Member of Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) Board. He is a social worker and the chairperson of Government school and PU college of Rajanukunte. He is the founder of Vishwa Vani Foundation.
